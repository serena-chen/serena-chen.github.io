Living Spaces, 2016
Promotional Flyers / 148 x 210 mm / Interactive App

Interactive app and flyers designed to promote a series of documentary films by Monash Architecture students investigating the experience of living, working and/or visiting Prato, Italy. Flyers were distributed around the city and public screenings of these short films took place on the 13th of June, 2016.
