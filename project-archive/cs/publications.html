We All Love Your Life
George Henry Longly, with essay by Victor Buchli
Editor with GHL, Copublished with CURA, 2017
Published on the occasion of the exhibition We All Love Your life at Red Bull Arts New York in 2016. The book brings together documentation of the exhibition, material generated retrospectively over the last two years, as well as the artist’s reference images from NASA. The structure of the book centers around a conversation between the artist and Victor Buchli, Professor of Material Culture at UCL.

Bjarne Melgaard, Babak Radboy
Red Bull Arts New York, Managing Editor, 2017
The size of a vinyl LP, the magazine takes the form of a boxed set, with each project individually bound within. Clouds of Petals
Sarah Meyohas, with essay by Jeppe Ugelvig
Red Bull Arts New York, Editor, 2016
On the occasion of Sarah Meyohas: Cloud of Petals, ﻿Red Bull Arts New York created and exhibition catalog and silk charmeuse scarf. This limited run features commissioned texts, photographs and stills providing insight to her multi-faceted practice. The contributing essays are written by Samuel Loncar and cultural critic and curator Jeppe Ugelvig. The scarf, printed digitally by Software Studios, is a limited edition of 25 pieces and features a still from her film Cloud of Petals (2016).
