Shrine To The Slogan, 2017
6 X Satin Wall-hangings / 841 x 594 mm
Woven Prayer Rug / 1350 x 950 mm

An installation series that glorifies the common mass-produced slogan T-shirt; a personal billboard and place for individual self-expression. Shrine to the Slogan is a sacred place for the lost and confused to come seek wisdom and find enlightenment. Wake up, praise, ponder, reflect, seek truth and then continue with your day.

*Exhibited at MADA Now 2017
